## Executive Summary

TALASH v3 is an AI-powered recruitment support system that automates CV screening, candidate profile analysis, and comprehensive evaluation beyond traditional keyword-based filtering. The system employs Large Language Models (LLMs) via Ollama (llama3.1) to extract structured information from unstructured CV documents and perform multi-dimensional candidate assessment.

**Milestone 1 Scope:** Completed preprocessing module, system architecture design, early prototype with folder-based CV ingestion, initial database schema, and working demo with preliminary extraction from sample CVs.
